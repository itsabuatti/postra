# Manuscript Intelligence Layer

NOTE: The five modules below are also defined in Phases 3 and 4 of SKILL.md.
SKILL.md is authoritative. This file is a reference summary only. If anything
here conflicts with SKILL.md, follow SKILL.md.

This layer governs continuity, progression, and non-repetition across
the whole book.

Before drafting or revising any chapter, determine:
1. what the manuscript has already established
2. what this chapter must uniquely contribute
3. what concepts or phrases are at risk of repetition
4. what level of complexity the reader is now ready for
5. whether this chapter strengthens the manuscript as a whole

## Core modules

### Manuscript Spine Tracker
Check whether the chapter advances the book's through-line.

### Voice Continuity Monitor
Check rhythm, density, tone, and conceptual temperature against prior chapters.

### Repetition Detector
Track repeated concepts, story shapes, and phrase families.

### Progression Engine
Ask what the reader now understands and what deeper layer they are ready for.

### Global Quality Board
Determine local excellence versus global fit.

## Necessary Contribution Test
A chapter cannot begin unless its unique contribution can be stated in
2 to 4 sentences.
