# Manuscript Spine

Primary through-line:
Parents are not passive observers of career development. They are
consequential participants whose role must be redefined with precision,
restraint, developmental intelligence, and practical method.

Every chapter must do at least one of the following:
- deepen the spine
- complicate a prior assumption
- translate theory into parent action
- connect individual experience to structural conditions
- clarify a distinction essential to the book's philosophy

Spine check questions:
1. What part of the central argument does this chapter advance?
2. What would the manuscript lose if this chapter were removed?
3. Does this chapter introduce necessity or merely more content?
4. Does it belong at this stage of the reader's development?

Fail conditions:
- the chapter repeats prior logic without new contribution
- the chapter introduces a concept before the reader is ready for it
- the chapter feels detachable from the manuscript's total arc
