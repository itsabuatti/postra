# Voice Lock

## Required qualities
- authoritative yet invitational
- rigorous yet accessible
- emotionally intelligent without sentimentality
- conceptually dense without obscurity
- warm without casual slackness

## Sentence rhythm
- vary sentence length deliberately
- blend analytic sentences with cleaner declarative lines
- avoid monotonous cadence

## Avoid
- inflated abstractions
- generic motivational language
- filler transitions
- mechanical list writing
- unexplained jargon
- decorative metaphor
- aviation framing of any kind

## Voice continuity check
Before finalizing, compare against prior chapters for:
- rhythm
- density
- temperature of voice
- clarity of definitions
- ratio of theory to lived example
